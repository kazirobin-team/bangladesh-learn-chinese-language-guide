Here are two example sentences for each Chinese word, with pinyin and English meaning:

---

爱 (ài – love)

1. 我爱我的妈妈。 – Wǒ ài wǒ de māma. – I love my mom.
2. 他很爱看电影。 – Tā hěn ài kàn diànyǐng. – He really loves watching movies.

八 (bā – eight)

1. 现在八点了。 – Xiànzài bā diǎn le. – It's eight o'clock now.
2. 我有八个苹果。 – Wǒ yǒu bā gè píngguǒ. – I have eight apples.

爸爸 (bàba – father)

1. 我爸爸是老师。 – Wǒ bàba shì lǎoshī. – My father is a teacher.
2. 爸爸在做饭。 – Bàba zài zuò fàn. – Dad is cooking.

杯子 (bēizi – cup)

1. 杯子在桌子上。 – Bēizi zài zhuōzi shàng. – The cup is on the table.
2. 这个杯子很漂亮。 – Zhège bēizi hěn piàoliang. – This cup is very pretty.

北京 (Běijīng – Beijing)

1. 北京是中国的首都。 – Běijīng shì Zhōngguó de shǒudū. – Beijing is the capital of China.
2. 我明年去北京。 – Wǒ míngnián qù Běijīng. – I'm going to Beijing next year.

本 (běn – measure word for books)

1. 我有一本书。 – Wǒ yǒu yī běn shū. – I have one book.
2. 这本词典很好。 – Zhè běn cídiǎn hěn hǎo. – This dictionary is very good.

不 (bù – not/no)

1. 我不喜欢吃辣。 – Wǒ bù xǐhuān chī là. – I don't like spicy food.
2. 他不是学生。 – Tā bù shì xuésheng. – He is not a student.

不客气 (bú kèqi – you're welcome)

1. 不客气，这是我应该做的。 – Bú kèqi, zhè shì wǒ yīnggāi zuò de. – You're welcome, this is what I should do.
2. 谢谢！ – 不客气！ – Xièxie! – Bú kèqi! – Thank you! – You're welcome!

菜 (cài – dish/food)

1. 这个菜很好吃。 – Zhège cài hěn hǎochī. – This dish is delicious.
2. 我妈妈做的菜很好。 – Wǒ māma zuò de cài hěn hǎo. – My mom's cooking is very good.

茶 (chá – tea)

1. 我要一杯茶。 – Wǒ yào yī bēi chá. – I'd like a cup of tea.
2. 中国茶很有名。 – Zhōngguó chá hěn yǒumíng. – Chinese tea is very famous.

吃 (chī – eat)

1. 你吃早饭了吗？ – Nǐ chī zǎofàn le ma? – Have you eaten breakfast?
2. 我想吃面条。 – Wǒ xiǎng chī miàntiáo. – I want to eat noodles.

出租车 (chūzūchē – taxi)

1. 我坐出租车去机场。 – Wǒ zuò chūzūchē qù jīchǎng. – I take a taxi to the airport.
2. 出租车在那边。 – Chūzūchē zài nàbiān. – The taxi is over there.

打电话 (dǎ diànhuà – make a phone call)

1. 我给你打电话。 – Wǒ gěi nǐ dǎ diànhuà. – I'll call you.
2. 他在打电话。 – Tā zài dǎ diànhuà. – He is making a phone call.

大 (dà – big)

1. 这个房间很大。 – Zhège fángjiān hěn dà. – This room is very big.
2. 北京是个大城市。 – Běijīng shì gè dà chéngshì. – Beijing is a big city.

的 (de – possessive particle)

1. 这是我的书。 – Zhè shì wǒ de shū. – This is my book.
2. 她是我朋友。 – Tā shì wǒ de péngyou. – She is my friend.

点 (diǎn – o'clock/point)

1. 现在三点。 – Xiànzài sān diǎn. – It's three o'clock.
2. 请你说慢一点。 – Qǐng nǐ shuō màn yī diǎn. – Please speak a little slower.

电脑 (diànnǎo – computer)

1. 我有一台电脑。 – Wǒ yǒu yī tái diànnǎo. – I have a computer.
2. 电脑坏了。 – Diànnǎo huài le. – The computer is broken.

电视 (diànshì – TV)

1. 我在看电视。 – Wǒ zài kàn diànshì. – I'm watching TV.
2. 电视声音太大了。 – Diànshì shēngyīn tài dà le. – The TV volume is too loud.

电影 (diànyǐng – movie)

1. 我喜欢看电影。 – Wǒ xǐhuān kàn diànyǐng. – I like watching movies.
2. 这个电影很好看。 – Zhège diànyǐng hěn hǎokàn. – This movie is very good.

东西 (dōngxi – thing)

1. 我买了些东西。 – Wǒ mǎi le xiē dōngxi. – I bought some things.
2. 东西在桌上。 – Dōngxi zài zhuō shàng. – The things are on the table.

都 (dōu – all)

1. 我们都去。 – Wǒmen dōu qù. – We all go.
2. 他们都很高。 – Tāmen dōu hěn gāo. – They are all very tall.

读 (dú – read)

1. 我每天读中文书。 – Wǒ měitiān dú Zhōngwén shū. – I read Chinese books every day.
2. 他在读报纸。 – Tā zài dú bàozhǐ. – He is reading the newspaper.

对不起 (duìbuqǐ – sorry)

1. 对不起，我迟到了。 – Duìbuqǐ, wǒ chídào le. – Sorry, I'm late.
2. 我对不起你。 – Wǒ duìbuqǐ nǐ. – I'm sorry to you.

多 (duō – many/much)

1. 这里人很多。 – Zhèlǐ rén hěn duō. – There are many people here.
2. 他有多高？ – Tā yǒu duō gāo? – How tall is he?

多少 (duōshao – how many)

1. 多少钱？ – Duōshao qián? – How much money?
2. 你有多少朋友？ – Nǐ yǒu duōshao péngyou? – How many friends do you have?

儿子 (érzi – son)

1. 我有一个儿子。 – Wǒ yǒu yī gè érzi. – I have one son.
2. 儿子今年十岁。 – Érzi jīnnián shí suì. – My son is ten years old this year.

二 (èr – two)

1. 我买两个。 – Wǒ mǎi liǎng gè. – I'll buy two.
2. 二加二等于四。 – Èr jiā èr děngyú sì. – Two plus two equals four.

饭店 (fàndiàn – restaurant)

1. 我们去饭店吃饭。 – Wǒmen qù fàndiàn chīfàn. – We're going to a restaurant to eat.
2. 这家饭店很贵。 – Zhè jiā fàndiàn hěn guì. – This restaurant is expensive.

飞机 (fēijī – airplane)

1. 我坐飞机去中国。 – Wǒ zuò fēijī qù Zhōngguó. – I take a plane to China.
2. 飞机在飞。 – Fēijī zài fēi. – The airplane is flying.

分钟 (fēnzhōng – minute)

1. 我等你十分钟。 – Wǒ děng nǐ shí fēnzhōng. – I'll wait for you for ten minutes.
2. 还有五分钟。 – Hái yǒu wǔ fēnzhōng. – There are five more minutes.
