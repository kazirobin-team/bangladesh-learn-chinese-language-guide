**✅ এখানে পুরো লিস্ট আবার দিলাম — এবার Hanzi + Pinyin + English সহ:**

---

**明天 – míngtiān – tomorrow**  
**明天我需要请一天假，因为我身体很不舒服。**  
**Míngtiān wǒ xūyào qǐng yī tiān jià, yīnwèi wǒ shēntǐ hěn bù shūfu.**  
**English:** Tomorrow I need to take a day off because I’m feeling very sick.

---

**今天 – jīntiān – today**  
**老板，今天办公室有5个人没来。**  
**Lǎobǎn, jīntiān bàngōngshì yǒu 5 gè rén méi lái.**  
**English:** Boss, 5 people didn’t come to the office today.

---

**多少 – duōshao – how many / how much**  
**你的手机多少钱？**  
**Nǐ de shǒujī duōshao qián?**  
**English:** How much is your phone?

---

**人 – rén – person**  
**我家里有5个人。**  
**Wǒ jiā li yǒu 5 gè rén.**  
**English:** There are 5 people in my family.

---

**认识 – rènshi – know (someone)**  
**我不认识你。**  
**Wǒ bù rènshi nǐ.**  
**English:** I don’t know you.

---

**什么 – shénme – what**  
**有什么问题？你知道什么？**  
**Yǒu shénme wèntí? Nǐ zhīdào shénme?**  
**English:** What’s the problem? What do you know?

---

**时候 – shíhou – time**  
**我们什么时候开始工作？**  
**Wǒmen shénme shíhou kāishǐ gōngzuò?**  
**English:** When will we start working?

---

**天气 – tiānqì – weather**  
**今天的天气很不好，下雨了。**  
**Jīntiān de tiānqì hěn bù hǎo, xià yǔ le.**  
**English:** Today’s weather is very bad. It rained.

---

**喜欢 – xǐhuan – like**  
**我喜欢你，因为你是我朋友。**  
**Wǒ xǐhuan nǐ, yīnwèi nǐ shì wǒ péngyou.**  
**English:** I like you because you are my friend.

---

**现在 – xiànzài – now**  
**我现在要出去。**  
**Wǒ xiànzài yào chūqù.**  
**English:** I’m going out now.

---

**以后 – yǐhòu – after / later**  
**这个工作我以后再做。**  
**Zhège gōngzuò wǒ yǐhòu zài zuò.**  
**English:** I’ll do this work later.
