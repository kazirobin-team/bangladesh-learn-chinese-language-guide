# HSK 1 Level - Advanced Vocabulary (Adjectives, Time, Location & Measure Words)

This document continues the HSK Level 1 vocabulary series with advanced adjectives, time expressions, location words, and measure words. Each entry includes complete pinyin pronunciation for both individual words and example sentences, Bengali transliteration, English translation, and Bengali meanings.

## Physical Adjectives & Age

### 101. 忙 (Busy)
- **Pinyin:** Máng
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** মাং
- **English Translation:** Busy
- **Example Sentence:** 我今天很忙。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ jīntiān hěn máng.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমি আজ খুব ব্যস্ত।

### 102. 累 (Tired)
- **Pinyin:** Lèi
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** লেই
- **English Translation:** Tired / Exhausted
- **Example Sentence:** 我很累。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ hěn lèi.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমি খুব ক্লান্ত।

### 103. 漂亮 (Beautiful)
- **Pinyin:** Piàoliang
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** পিয়াও লিয়াং
- **English Translation:** Beautiful / Pretty
- **Example Sentence:** 她很漂亮。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Tā hěn piàoliang.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** সে খুব সুন্দর।

### 104. 高 (Tall / High)
- **Pinyin:** Gāo
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** গাও
- **English Translation:** Tall / High
- **Example Sentence:** 他很高。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Tā hěn gāo.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** সে অনেক লম্বা।

### 105. 矮 (Short / Shorter)
- **Pinyin:** Ǎi
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** আই
- **English Translation:** Short / Shorter in height
- **Example Sentence:** 他有点矮。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Tā yǒudiǎn ǎi.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** সে একটু খাটো।

### 106. 岁 (Years of Age)
- **Pinyin:** Suì
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** সুই
- **English Translation:** Years old / Age
- **Example Sentence:** 我二十岁。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ èrshí suì.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার বয়স ২০ বছর।

## Time Expressions (Periods & Seasons)

### 107. 月 (Month / Moon)
- **Pinyin:** Yuè
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** ইউয়ে
- **English Translation:** Month / Moon
- **Example Sentence:** 这个月很忙。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Zhège yuè hěn máng.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** এই মাস খুব ব্যস্ত।

### 108. 日 (Day / Date)
- **Pinyin:** Rì
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** রি
- **English Translation:** Day / Date
- **Example Sentence:** 今天是好日子。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Jīntiān shì hǎo rìzi.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আজ ভালো দিন।

### 109. 星期 (Week)
- **Pinyin:** Xīngqī
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** শিং ছি
- **English Translation:** Week
- **Example Sentence:** 一个星期有七天。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Yī ge xīngqī yǒu qī tiān.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** এক সপ্তাহে সাত দিন।

### 110. 年 (Year)
- **Pinyin:** Nián
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** নিয়েন
- **English Translation:** Year
- **Example Sentence:** 我学习中文一年了。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ xuéxí Zhōngwén yī nián le.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমি এক বছর ধরে চাইনিজ শিখছি।

## Location Words (Directions & Positions)

### 115. 前面 (Front / Ahead)
- **Pinyin:** Qiánmiàn
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** ছিয়ান মিয়ান
- **English Translation:** Front / Ahead / In front of
- **Example Sentence:** 学校在前面。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Xuéxiào zài qiánmiàn.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** স্কুল সামনে।

### 116. 后面 (Back / Behind)
- **Pinyin:** Hòumiàn
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** হৌ মিয়ান
- **English Translation:** Back / Behind
- **Example Sentence:** 我家在学校后面。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ jiā zài xuéxiào hòumiàn.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার বাড়ি স্কুলের পিছনে।

### 117. 里面 (Inside)
- **Pinyin:** Lǐmiàn
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** লি মিয়ান
- **English Translation:** Inside / Interior
- **Example Sentence:** 房间里面有人。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Fángjiān lǐmiàn yǒu rén.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** রুমের ভিতরে মানুষ আছে।

### 118. 外面 (Outside)
- **Pinyin:** Wàimiàn
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** ওয়াই মিয়ান
- **English Translation:** Outside / Exterior
- **Example Sentence:** 外面很冷。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wàimiàn hěn lěng.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** বাইরে খুব ঠান্ডা।

### 119. 上 (Up / On / Above)
- **Pinyin:** Shàng
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** শাং
- **English Translation:** Up / On / Above
- **Example Sentence:** 书在桌子上。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Shū zài zhuōzi shàng.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** বই টেবিলের উপরে আছে।

### 120. 下 (Down / Below / Under)
- **Pinyin:** Xià
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** শিয়া
- **English Translation:** Down / Below / Under
- **Example Sentence:** 猫在桌子下。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Māo zài zhuōzi xià.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** বিড়াল টেবিলের নিচে আছে।

## Measure Words & Quantifiers

### 121. 里 (Inside / In)
- **Pinyin:** Lǐ
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** লি
- **English Translation:** Inside / Within / In
- **Example Sentence:** 我在房间里。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ zài fángjiān lǐ.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমি রুমের ভিতরে আছি।

### 122. 个 (Classifier for General Objects)
- **Pinyin:** Gè
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** গা
- **English Translation:** Classifier / Measure word (general)
- **Example Sentence:** 我有一个朋友。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ yǒu yī ge péngyou.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার একজন বন্ধু আছে।

### 123. 本 (Classifier for Books)
- **Pinyin:** Běn
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** বান
- **English Translation:** Classifier for books
- **Example Sentence:** 我有一本书。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ yǒu yī běn shū.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার একটি বই আছে।

### 124. 块 (Classifier for Money / Pieces)
- **Pinyin:** Kuài
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** খুয়াই
- **English Translation:** Classifier for money / pieces
- **Example Sentence:** 这个苹果五块钱。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Zhège píngguǒ wǔ kuài qián.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** এই আপেলের দাম পাঁচ টাকা।

### 125. 些 (Some / A few)
- **Pinyin:** Xiē
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** শিয়ে
- **English Translation:** Some / A few
- **Example Sentence:** 我有一些问题。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ yǒu yīxiē wèntí.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার কিছু প্রশ্ন আছে।

## Study Tips

- **Vocabulary Organization:** This advanced section covers physical adjectives, temporal expressions, spatial/location vocabulary, and measure words—all essential for more complex sentence construction.
- **Contextual Learning:** Each entry includes practical examples that show how these words are used in real-world situations.
- **Measure Words:** Pay special attention to measure words (classifiers) like 个, 本, and 块, as they are crucial in Chinese grammar when counting objects.
- **Location & Prepositions:** Master location words to describe where things are, which is fundamental for giving directions and describing spatial relationships.
- **Time Management:** These time-related words will help you discuss schedules, daily routines, and plan activities in Chinese.
