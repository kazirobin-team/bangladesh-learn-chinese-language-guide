# HSK 1 Level - Vocabulary (Professions, Time, Verbs & Actions)

This document continues the HSK Level 1 vocabulary series, featuring professions, time-related vocabulary, and essential verbs and actions. Each entry includes complete pinyin pronunciation for both individual words and example sentences, Bengali transliteration, English translation, and Bengali meanings.

## Professions & Work

### 51. 医生 (Doctor)
- **Pinyin:** Yīshēng
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** ই শাং
- **English Translation:** Doctor / Physician
- **Example Sentence:** 他是医生。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Tā shì yīshēng.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** সে একজন ডাক্তার।

### 52. 工作 (Work / Job)
- **Pinyin:** Gōngzuò
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** গং জুয়া
- **English Translation:** Work / Job / Employment
- **Example Sentence:** 我有很多工作。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ yǒu hěnduō gōngzuò.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার অনেক কাজ আছে।

### 53. 钱 (Money)
- **Pinyin:** Qián
- **Bengali Pronunciation:** ছিয়ান
- **English Translation:** Money / Cash
- **Example Sentence:** 我没有钱。
  - **Sentence Pinyin:** Wǒ méiyǒu qián.
  - **Bengali Meaning:** আমার টাকা নেই।

## Study Tips

- **Vocabulary Organization:** This section covers essential verbs and actions commonly used in daily conversations, including work-related vocabulary, time expressions, and common activities.
- **Contextual Learning:** Each verb is presented with practical example sentences that demonstrate real-world usage.
- **Pronunciation Practice:** Practice the pinyin pronunciations with careful attention to tone marks, as tone is crucial in Chinese.
- **Verb Combinations:** These verbs can be combined with objects (nouns from previous sections) to form complete meaningful sentences.
