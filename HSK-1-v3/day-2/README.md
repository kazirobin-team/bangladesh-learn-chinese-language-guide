**Here is the clean and organized version:**

### 1. First: The 10 Words

1. **吃** – chī – eat  
2. **出租车** – chūzūchē – taxi  
3. **打电话** – dǎ diànhuà – make a phone call  
4. **大** – dà – big  
5. **的** – de – possessive particle  
6. **点** – diǎn – o’clock / point  
7. **电脑** – diànnǎo – computer  
8. **电视** – diànshì – TV  
9. **电影** – diànyǐng – movie  
10. **东西** – dōngxi – thing / stuff

---

### 2. Each Word with Example Sentence

1. **吃** (chī) – eat  
   **Sentence:** 我喜欢吃米饭。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǒ xǐhuan chī mǐfàn.  
   **English:** I like to eat rice.

2. **出租车** (chūzūchē) – taxi  
   **Sentence:** 我坐出租车去机场。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǒ zuò chūzūchē qù jīchǎng.  
   **English:** I take a taxi to the airport.

3. **打电话** (dǎ diànhuà) – make a phone call  
   **Sentence:** 我要给妈妈打电话。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǒ yào gěi māma dǎ diànhuà.  
   **English:** I want to make a phone call to mom.

4. **大** (dà) – big  
   **Sentence:** 这个房子很大。  
   **Pinyin:** Zhège fángzi hěn dà.  
   **English:** This house is very big.

5. **的** (de) – possessive particle  
   **Sentence:** 这是我的电脑。  
   **Pinyin:** Zhè shì wǒ de diànnǎo.  
   **English:** This is my computer.

6. **点** (diǎn) – o’clock / point  
   **Sentence:** 现在是三点。  
   **Pinyin:** Xiànzài shì sān diǎn.  
   **English:** It is three o’clock now.

7. **电脑** (diànnǎo) – computer  
   **Sentence:** 我每天用电脑工作。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǒ měitiān yòng diànnǎo gōngzuò.  
   **English:** I use the computer for work every day.

8. **电视** (diànshì) – TV  
   **Sentence:** 晚上我喜欢看电视。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǎnshang wǒ xǐhuan kàn diànshì.  
   **English:** I like to watch TV in the evening.

9. **电影** (diànyǐng) – movie  
   **Sentence:** 我们一起去看电影吧。  
   **Pinyin:** Wǒmen yìqǐ qù kàn diànyǐng ba.  
   **English:** Let’s go watch a movie together.

10. **东西** (dōngxi) – thing / stuff  
    **Sentence:** 我买了很多东西。  
    **Pinyin:** Wǒ mǎi le hěn duō dōngxi.  
    **English:** I bought a lot of things.
